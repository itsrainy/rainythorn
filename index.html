Our Story

Rainy and Thorn met four years ago and have been sharing their Lawrenceville home with their two cats, Ginkgo and Willow, for almost three years now. When Thorn was ready to propose, they orchestrated the perfect surprise by having all their friends to pretend they were hosting a "wear the thing you never get a chance to wear" party—everyone was in on it except Rainy!

They love to travel together and work on creative projects together when they're at home. Thorn designs and sews clothes and is constantly making Rainy new outfits (and mending her old ones). They are actually making both of their outfits for the wedding!

Rainy likes creating electronic art pieces, biking, and playing music with her best friend (and maid of honor) Aubrey.

Together they love to spend time at home with their cats and two or three (or sometimes 12) of their favorite people. They also like to spend time out of the house with their friends grabbing drinks at breweries, playing pinball, and going camping. They're so excited to have a big celebration of love and community with all of their people.

Help Us Celebrate!

We believe that weddings should be a communal event; not just a celebration of our love for each other but a celebration of the community around us and the love that we all share.

To that end, we would love for everyone to participate and contribute in some way! Do you want to craft some decorations? Make some cookies? Read a poem? Present a humorous powerpoint? There are so many ways to contribute and we would love to harness some of the creativity of all our favorite people (that's you!)

If you have something in mind or have an idea you want to run by us, please reach out!
